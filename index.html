The problem

Godrej Jersey's curd packaging line ran at just 44% OEE. The team was missing demand targets daily with zero visibility into why. Through user research and customer journey mapping, I identified delayed downtime classification as the root cause — ₹2.8Cr/qtr was bleeding out silently because "planned" downtimes weren't tracked for overruns.

The problem

Operators were classifying downtime reasons one machine at a time — slow, repetitive, and error-prone. When the same downtime applied to multiple machines, there was no way to assign it in bulk. This caused data gaps that masked production losses.

What I did

  • Ran user interviews to identify bulk assignment as the highest-friction job-to-be-done
  • Authored PRD, user stories, and acceptance criteria using MoSCoW prioritisation
  • Shipped bulk assignment, range-selection, and Select All across 8 machines
  • Applied RCA + risk mitigation — shipped Merge & Unmerge with full audit trail
  • Ran usability testing pre-launch; tracked KPIs post-rollout
